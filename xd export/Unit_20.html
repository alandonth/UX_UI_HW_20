In the beginning...
What'sHapp was like any other travel app. A place for the user to organize dates and set itineraries with the added ability to see events or attractions taking place in specific areas. We knew there had to be more, so we did some...
Research
"I'm the type of person that can plan out 60% of the trip and purposely leave 40% of it unplanned to be spontaneous. " - Vishal P.
Our research began by conducting face to face interviews in search of qualitative data as well as distributing a survey for more quantitative data. We asked questions that gave us valuable insight into the mindset of modern travelers. After we listened, we began to analyze and synthesize our data utilizing our user insight board, affinity diagram, and empathy map. It wasn't long before we discovered that our users weren't looking for a way to plan their vacations but almost a way to "not plan out all of their vacations".
People don't want to their vacation to feel like work. They want the freedom and flexibility in their schedule to explore and be spontaneous while still taking comfort in the fact that what they book will be worth it.
